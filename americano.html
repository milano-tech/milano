Americano Coffee Recipe

Method:

Step 1

Grind for a double shot of espresso.

Step 2

Pour a short double espresso (2oz) into a 6-8 oz glass.

Step 3

Pour 4-6 oz of hot water over the espresso.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 espresso
  • 2/3 hot water
